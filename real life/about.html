About BakeRoute

Welcome to BakeRoute, Kampala's premier online destination for discovering, ordering, and enjoying the finest cakes and pastries. We bridge the gap between talented local bakers and dessert lovers, ensuring every celebration is paired with the perfect sweet treat.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower independent bakers by providing them with a platform to showcase their craft, while offering customers an effortless, delightful, and reliable way to satisfy their sweet cravings with fast delivery directly to their doorstep.

Why Choose Us?

  • Curated Quality: We partner only with top-rated, passionate bakers.
  • Seamless Ordering: A smooth, professional, and easy-to-use platform.
  • Lightning Fast Delivery: Freshness guaranteed, right on time.
  • Support Local: Every order helps a local business grow.
